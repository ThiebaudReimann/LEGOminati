LEGOminati

Hi! We are LEGOminati from WWG Bayreuth.
As a team, we participate in the international competition of the FLL (First Lego League) every year. Our team consists of students from 7th to 12th grade. We meet once a week on Friday afternoons for 1:30 hours to prepare for the upcoming competition. However, there is no strict time limit, and we often continue working, even beyond that time, either at school or at home during our free time.

Team

FIRST® LEGO® League

The First Lego League is an international competition. There is a new season theme every year. In the Challenge age group, in which our team competes every year, children and teenagers aged 9 to 16 can take part. The aim of this competition is to deal with technology and the future. The competition is divided into various main categories: Robot Game on the Field, Robot Design and Research. Each team must prepare in all areas and then present what they have created over the last few months on the day of the competition.
